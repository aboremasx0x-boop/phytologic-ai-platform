# 🌿 Phytologic AI
Smart Agricultural Early Warning Platform

----------------------------------------

## OVERVIEW

Phytologic AI is an intelligent agricultural platform designed to detect plant diseases early, analyze agricultural risks, and support decision-making using Artificial Intelligence, geospatial mapping, and real-time environmental data.

The platform integrates:

- AI plant disease diagnosis
- Real-time weather analysis
- Disease spread prediction
- Interactive agricultural maps
- National monitoring dashboards
- Operational alert systems

The goal is to reduce crop loss, improve agricultural monitoring, and support food security.

----------------------------------------

## KEY FEATURES

### 1) AI PLANT DISEASE DIAGNOSIS

Farmers upload plant images and the system uses AI models to:

- Identify plant diseases
- Calculate prediction confidence
- Estimate disease severity
- Generate treatment recommendations

----------------------------------------

### 2) AGRICULTURAL RISK FORECASTING

The platform analyzes:

- Temperature
- Humidity
- Rainfall
- Historical disease records

to calculate a disease risk score and classify the risk level as:

- Low
- Medium
- High

----------------------------------------

### 3) INTERACTIVE AGRICULTURAL MAP

The system displays reported cases on a live geospatial map, allowing decision-makers to:

- Visualize infection distribution
- Detect hotspots
- Monitor regional disease trends

----------------------------------------

### 4) NATIONAL MONITORING CENTER

The National Center Dashboard provides a high-level view including:

- national disease statistics
- most affected regions
- risk trends across the country

This helps government agencies monitor agricultural health.

----------------------------------------

### 5) REAL-TIME OPERATIONS CENTER

The Operations Center allows real-time monitoring of:

- disease alerts
- active cases
- emerging agricultural threats

This module supports fast operational response.

----------------------------------------

### 6) AI FORECAST MODULE

The platform includes an AI forecasting system that predicts future disease risk based on:

- environmental conditions
- case history
- severity indicators
